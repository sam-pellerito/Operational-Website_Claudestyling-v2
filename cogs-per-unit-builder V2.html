Section 2 — Cost accounting Variable vs fixed split

Separates variable costs (change with volume) from fixed costs (constant regardless of output). Essential for pricing decisions, break-even analysis, and understanding contribution margin.

Variable costs
Costs that scale directly with every unit produced. Materials and packaging from Section 1 are carried over automatically.
Fixed costs (allocated per unit at batch size)
Costs that don't change with volume — allocated to a per-unit figure based on your batch size. As volume rises, these shrink per unit.
