Trade Spend & Net Revenue

Calculate true net revenue and gross margin after trade spend, platform fees, fulfillment, distributor margins, and other channel deductions. See what you actually keep from each dollar of gross revenue.

Use this before approving a promotion, comparing sales channels, reviewing retailer deductions, or deciding whether a channel is actually profitable.

Built by Sam Pellerito

How to use this tool

  1. Select your channel — default deduction rates load automatically for retail, DTC, Amazon, or foodservice.
  2. Enter gross revenue — total invoiced before any deductions or allowances.
  3. Enter COGS — product-level cost only. Do not include fulfillment, platform fees, or freight if those are already entered as channel deductions below.
  4. Adjust deductions — use the defaults or enter your actual spend. Toggle between $ and % of gross.
  5. Click Calculate to see net revenue, gross margin, and a side-by-side channel comparison.
