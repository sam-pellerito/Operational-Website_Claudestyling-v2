Cash-to-Cash Cycle

DIO + DSO − DPO — how many days cash is trapped in your operations before you collect from customers. The cash conversion cycle is the single biggest driver of working capital for CPG brands.

The three components

📦 DIO

Days Inventory Outstanding — how long product sits before it's sold.

= Avg Inventory ÷ COGS × 365

🧾 DSO

Days Sales Outstanding — how long before customers pay you.

= AR ÷ Revenue × 365

🤝 DPO

Days Payable Outstanding — how long before you pay your suppliers.

= AP ÷ COGS × 365
